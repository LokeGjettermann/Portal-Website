Welcome to Portal

Portal is an award-winning puzzle game where you play as Chell, navigating through a series of challenging rooms using a unique portal gun. This gun's ability to create portals between two surfaces allows you to solve complex puzzles, overcome obstacles, and creatively manipulate the laws of physics. With its innovative gameplay, and thought-provoking story, Portal offers an unforgettable experience that challenges both your logic and problem-solving skills.
